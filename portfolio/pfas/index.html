Case Study - PFAS Occurence Study for Planned Pilot Projects


USE CASE: Drinking Water, Regulatory Compliance


BACKGROUND

To prepare for forthcoming state and federal regulations, a water utility first had to conduct an enterprise-wide PFAS occurence study to evaluate the presence of various PFAS analytes. across their entire footprint.

OUTCOME: A targeted view of PFAS occurence and identification of sites for pilot studies

Working with other water quality Scientists, Aaron assisted the team in visualizing and analyzing data from the study, and helped determine key targets for ion exchange pilot projects.
